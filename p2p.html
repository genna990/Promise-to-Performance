📜 White Paper on Local Government Review — Clause Alignment 8 clauses addressed ▼ expand
SALGA is currently reviewing the 1998 White Paper on Local Government — the most significant policy rewrite since democracy. The Municipal P2P Dashboard directly addresses 8 of the priority reform clauses. This positions the system as a policy implementation tool, not just a technology product.
Chapter 4 — Community Participation in Local Government
"Municipalities must develop mechanisms for communities to participate in the affairs of the municipality." The Data Steward verification model and citizen feedback on every promise directly fulfils this obligation.
✓ FULFILLED
Chapter 5 — Ward Committees and Councillor Accountability
"Ward councillors must be accountable to their communities for the commitments they make." The leaderboard, promise tracking and discrepancy flagging creates this accountability infrastructure for the first time.
✓ FULFILLED
Chapter 6 — IDP as a Living Document
"The IDP must be implemented through measurable, time-bound commitments at ward level." All promises in this system are tagged to IDP priority areas with deadlines and budget tracking.
✓ FULFILLED
Chapter 8 — Performance Management Systems
"Municipalities must develop performance management systems that track service delivery at ward level." The accountability score and IDP progress tracker constitute exactly this system.
✓ FULFILLED
Chapter 9 — Financial Management and MFMA Compliance
"Budget spending must be traceable to service delivery outcomes at ward level." The budget tracking per promise and Section 71 auto-alerts create this traceability.
✓ FULFILLED
Chapter 11 — Digital Governance and E-Participation
"Municipalities should leverage technology to improve citizen engagement and transparency." This dashboard works on any device, offline in rural dead zones, requiring no app installation.
✓ FULFILLED
Chapter 3 — Rural Municipality Differentiation
"Rural municipalities face unique connectivity and capacity constraints that must be addressed in governance systems." The Data Steward model and satellite-linked offline operation is built specifically for rural dead zones like Kuboes and Nourivier.
✓ FULFILLED
Chapter 7 — Intergovernmental Relations
"District municipalities must coordinate service delivery accountability across local municipalities." The cross-municipality comparison and district-level reporting enables this coordination for SALGA, COGTA and provincial government.
✓ FULFILLED
