Who's Keeping Their Word?

Our Mission

Bridging the Gap Between Promises and Reality

The Promise-To-Performance Dashboard brings transparency to local governance. Every promise made by a public official is a commitment to the community — this platform holds them to it.

✓Digital archive of every manifesto and speech
✓Real-time tracking of service delivery projects
✓Independent data steward verification layer
✓Community feedback directly on each promise
✓Data-driven leaderboards to encourage performance
